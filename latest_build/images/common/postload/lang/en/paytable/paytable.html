ABOUT THE GAME

NEPTUNE RISING is played with 10 fixed paylines. The game has eleven regular symbols, one Wild and one Scatter.


NEPTUNE RISING FEATURE

Neptune Rising Feature can trigger randomly on a non-winning spin. During the feature, Neptune rises from the ocean and reveals the required symbols to form a win.

On a near miss scatter spin, randomly, Neptune will rise from 1 or more of the other 3 reels and reveal 1, 2 or 3 further scatter symbols to award the Free Spins.

On a losing near miss spin of any symbol, randomly, Neptune will rise from 1 or more of the other reels and reveal the required symbols to create a winning combination.


FREE SPINS FEATURE

3 or more Coin symbols anywhere trigger 10 Free Spins.

Any symbol is picked at random for Neptune Rising Feature.

Neptune rises from the ocean to reveal the random symbol on all positions of reel 1 and 2 for 2 of a kind wins or reels 1, 2 and 3 for 3 of a kind wins. 9, 10, Dolphin, Treasure Chest, Scatter and Wild are picked for 2 of a kind wins. Q, K, A, Neptune, Siren and Sea horse are picked for 3 of a kind wins. The remaining reels spin and reveal the final win. Free Spins can be retriggered.
